During my predoctoral period, I taught problem classes at the Department of Mathematics at the Autonomous University of Madrid during the winter semesters of the 2022-2023 and 2023-2024 academic years. I taught Mathematics for the degrees in Biochemistry and Food Science and Technology, as well as Mathematics I (Linear Algebra) for the degree in Biomedical Engineering.
