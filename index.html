About me

I am a mathematical physicist and differential geometer, currently holding a predoctoral researcher position at the Institute of Mathematical Sciences (ICMAT) in Madrid, Spain. I have written my PhD dissertation, titled The geometry of dissipation, under the supervision of Prof. Manuel de León. Following the defense of my thesis, scheduled for September 18, 2024, I will be joining the Institute of Mathematics of the Polish Academy of Sciences (IM PAN) in Warsaw, Poland, as an assistant professor, under the mentorship of Prof. Janusz Grabowski.
